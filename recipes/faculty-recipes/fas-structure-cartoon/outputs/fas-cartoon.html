Mammalian Fatty Acid Synthase (FAS) — Catalytic Domains

A single multifunctional assembly-line enzyme (homodimer; primary product: palmitate, C16). Each domain runs one step of the cycle.

KSKetoacyl synthase — Claisen condensation; adds 2 carbons (elongation).
ATAcyl transferase (MAT) — selects acetyl-/malonyl-CoA, loads it onto ACP.
DHDehydratase — eliminates the –OH as water to form the enoyl thioester.
EREnoyl reductase — reduces the α,β-double bond (NADPH).
KRKetoreductase — reduces the new β-ketone to a hydroxyl (NADPH).
ACPAcyl carrier protein — shuttles the growing chain between domains.
TEThioesterase — termination: hydrolyzes the thioester to release the finished palmitate.
